# The Unexpected Girl 

Met a girl recently, or should I say a warrior wearing her unbreakable armour of smile and humor. The thing is her armour is very strong but there is a chink in her armour, her memories. 

The good thing is that she knows this and handles this pretty well and always has a training ground in the form of a best friend through which she can discuss everything and I am pretty sure that if this continues, she will get better at her training and the chink in her armour will be gone.

The bad thing is, while testing her armour, when I found the chink it was unexpected to her and that I think broke a part of the armour which was not good.This was a bad decision from my side to test her armour when I came to know that she has a best friend as testing ground.

When that girl is with people who don't know about her armour, she is very happy and I think that is very good for her for now.

**We are stopping this book, as the Protagonist is still fighting her demons and trying to be happy, so we will begin this once she reaches that point.**

Hopefully we will write this book one day, but that day is not today, so we will wait for her to contact me and talk.
